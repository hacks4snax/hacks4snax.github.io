Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

Applies to apps and services published by Hacks4Snax.

Summary: Hacks4Snax apps are designed to collect as little information as possible. Many app features run on your device. We do not sell personal information, use cross-app advertising tracking, or require an account unless an app clearly says otherwise.

Information we collect

Our apps do not require you to create an account by default. If an app does not ask you to enter contact information, we do not receive contact information from that app.

Some apps may process content you choose, such as photos, files, text, or documents. Unless the app clearly states that cloud or online processing is being used, that content is processed on your device and is not sent to a Hacks4Snax-operated backend.

Photos, files, and user content

When an app uses Apple system pickers for photos or files, the app receives only the items you select. The app does not receive general access to your full library unless you grant that access through iOS permissions.

Purchases

If an app offers in-app purchases, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details. Apple may provide purchase status to the app so paid features can be enabled or restored.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Our apps do not use third-party advertising SDKs or cross-app advertising tracking. If a future app uses analytics or crash reporting, that app’s App Store privacy details will describe the data involved.

Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate an app feature you request, comply with law, prevent abuse, or complete App Store purchase functionality through Apple.

Children’s privacy

Our apps are general-purpose utilities. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if our apps or data practices change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact support@hacks4snacks.com.
